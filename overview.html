TITLE AND REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS

Trailers that weigh more than 2,500 lbs., as well as all trailer coaches, must have a title issued by the Secretary of State. Michigan towing laws require all trailers, regardless of weight, to be registered and display a valid license plate. Registration fees are based on the weight of the trailer.License plates issued for trailers are permanent and non-transferable.
